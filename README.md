The smarter way to pay—so you can stay golden.

Innovative first-hand idea by Shelby Nicolle Isaacs  
Concept timestamp: June 23, 2025 – 11:51 a.m.

🔍 What URGLDN solves
URGLDN addresses one of the most common and costly financial pain points Canadians face:
bills that don’t line up with income.

Millions of people aren’t struggling because they don’t earn enough — they’re struggling because:

A payment comes out a day too early

Something gets forgotten

They accidentally overspend without realizing a bill is coming

This leads to overdraft fees, late fees, damaged credit, and constant stress.

Existing tools — reminders, calendars, and auto‑pay — either rely on user discipline or risk pulling money when the account isn’t ready.

URGLDN changes that by focusing on timing, not just tracking.

💡 The URGLDN concept
URGLDN acts like a virtual banking assistant that organizes bill timing ahead of time.

Users lock bill money in advance in a protected “vault”

Funds are shielded from accidental spending

URGLDN releases the money back to the main account exactly when the bill is due

It functions like auto‑pay, but smarter:
instead of pulling funds blindly on the due date, URGLDN locks the money ahead of time and releases it at the right moment.

Members get the peace of mind of knowing their bills are handled early—without the fear of missed or short payments and the consequences that follow.

🎯 Ideal users
URGLDN is built for:

People whose bills don’t line up cleanly with their income

Monthly or irregular income earners

Precarious‑income households

Gig workers, students, and first‑time budgeters

Families, small businesses, and landlords with recurring payments

Anyone tired of overdraft surprises and timing stress

🛠 Core mechanism – Lock & Release
Lock‑and‑Release Mechanism
Funds are reserved ahead of time

Money is protected from accidental spending

Payment is pin‑pointed and released on the right day, at the right time

This:

Eliminates many late fees

Reduces overdrafts

Helps protect and improve credit scores

🧩 Integration model – built for banks & credit unions
URGLDN is designed to plug directly into existing banking apps as a white‑label feature:

Sits on top of the bank’s existing accounts and payment rails

Uses the institution’s trust, security, and login

Feels like a native feature inside the bank’s own app

User benefits

Peace of mind

Reduced financial stress

No more “surprise” payments

Bank benefits

Stronger member loyalty

Differentiation in a competitive market

⭐ Why URGLDN is different
Unlike reminder apps or standard auto‑pay:

URGLDN protects funds instead of just notifying users

It prevents overdrafts instead of causing them

It manages timing proactively instead of reacting after the fact

It works with the bank’s existing infrastructure

This makes URGLDN:

Safer than auto‑pay (which often causes the most stress)

More effective than reminders (because the money is already locked and scheduled to be released)
