📄 URGLDN – Executive Summary (Bank‑Ready Version)
URGLDN is a financial wellness tool designed to integrate directly into existing banking apps. It functions like auto‑pay, but smarter: instead of pulling funds blindly on the due date, URGLDN locks the money ahead of time and releases it exactly when the bill is due. This eliminates late fees, reduces overdrafts, and gives users the peace of mind of knowing their bills are handled before they even think about them.

Think of URGLDN as a virtual banking assistant that safeguards funds, organizes bill timing, and prevents the stress and consequences of missed or short payments.

⭐ The Problem
Canadians pay millions every year in late fees, overdraft charges, and penalties caused by:

Bills that don’t line up with income

Forgetting due dates

Overspending before a payment hits

Auto‑pay pulling funds when the account isn’t ready

Existing tools — reminders, calendars, and auto‑pay — rely on user discipline or risk overdrafts.

Banks are actively seeking solutions that improve customer satisfaction, reduce complaints, and strengthen loyalty.

⭐ Why URGLDN Is Different
Unlike reminder apps or auto‑pay systems:

URGLDN protects funds instead of just notifying users

It prevents overdrafts instead of causing them

It manages timing proactively instead of reacting after the fact

It works with the bank’s existing infrastructure

This makes URGLDN safer than auto‑pay, which often causes the most stress, and more effective than reminders, since funds are already locked and scheduled to be released exactly when needed.
