URGLDN — Licensing, Royalty & Equity Deal Structure (Prototype Stage)
URGLDN is a financial‑wellness automation concept designed to help users time their bills, manage cash flow, and reduce financial stress.
A semi‑functional prototype has been created to demonstrate the core logic and user experience.
This document outlines the proposed licensing, royalty, and equity structure for institutional partners interested in developing URGLDN into a full production product.

🧠 1. Ownership
100% ownership of URGLDN (concept, workflows, logic, and IP) remains with the inventor, Shelby Isaacs.

Licensing grants usage rights only, not ownership or control of the IP.

The current prototype is proof‑of‑concept only, not a production system.

🏦 2. Licensing Rights
Partners receive:

Rights to build, develop, engineer, integrate, and scale URGLDN into a full production product.

Permission to white‑label URGLDN under their brand.

Access to prototype logic, workflow diagrams, and user‑tested sequences (post‑NDA).

Ability to embed URGLDN into banking, payroll, or fintech ecosystems.

Licensing options:

Non‑Exclusive License (standard pricing)

Exclusive License (premium pricing, limited by region or market segment)

💰 3. Royalty Structure
Option A — Revenue‑Based Royalty
5% of gross revenue generated by URGLDN or URGLDN‑powered features.

Option B — User‑Based Royalty
$0.25–$1.00 per Monthly Active User (MAU) using URGLDN features.

Option C — Hybrid Model
Lower per‑user fee + lower revenue percentage.

Royalty payments are made quarterly.

💵 4. Upfront Licensing Fee
To secure rights and cover transfer of prototype logic and IP:

$50,000–$150,000 upfront licensing fee  
(varies by exclusivity, integration scope, and distribution scale)

📈 5. Equity Component (Optional but Recommended)
Partners may receive equity in URGLDN to support development, compliance, and scaling.

Equity Range
5–15% equity, vested through milestones.

Milestone‑Based Vesting
Equity vests only when the partner completes key deliverables such as:

Full production build of URGLDN

Integration into their ecosystem

Public launch to users

Achieving adoption thresholds

This ensures equity rewards real progress, not promises.

🔒 6. Exclusivity Options
Non‑Exclusive License
Standard royalty

Standard upfront fee

Multiple institutions may license URGLDN

Exclusive License
Higher upfront fee

Higher minimum royalty guarantee

Exclusive rights for a defined region or market segment

📅 7. Term Length
5–10 year licensing term, renewable

Royalty obligations continue for the full term

📝 8. NDA Requirement
Before receiving:

Prototype logic

Workflow diagrams

User‑tested sequences

Technical explanation of the Lock & Release™ mechanism

A mutual NDA is required.

🛠️ 9. Support & Involvement
The partner is responsible for:

Full development and engineering

Compliance and regulatory requirements

Security and infrastructure

Scaling and maintenance

The inventor (Shelby Isaacs) has:

No operational involvement

No development responsibilities

No staffing or management obligations

The inventor receives:

Royalties

Optional equity

Licensing fees

🧩 10. Summary
URGLDN is a prototype‑stage financial‑wellness concept offered under a clean, scalable licensing model.
Partners receive the rights to build and scale the product, while the inventor retains ownership and receives royalties and optional equity.

This structure protects the IP, rewards real development progress, and creates a long‑term revenue stream for both parties.
