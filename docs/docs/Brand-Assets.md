# URGLDN – Branding Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity Overview
URGLDN is a timing‑first financial wellness tool.  
The brand must always communicate:

- Protection  
- Precision  
- Simplicity  
- Financial confidence  
- “Stay Golden” energy  

URGLDN is calm, structured, and trustworthy — a shield, a lock, and a steady hand guiding users toward financial peace.

---

## 2. Official Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Gold** — #D4AF37  
  Represents value, timing, and the “stay golden” identity.

- **Silver** — #C0C0C0  
  Clean, modern, trustworthy.

- **Black** — #000000  
  Strength, clarity, precision.

- **White** — #FFFFFF  
  Simplicity, transparency, ease.

### Accent Color
- **Green** — #2ECC71  
  Financial wellness, success, positive outcomes.

**Usage Notes:**
- Gold is the hero color for highlights and emphasis.  
- Black + white form the clean base.  
- Silver supports the modern fintech aesthetic.  
- Green is used sparingly for success states and confirmations.
