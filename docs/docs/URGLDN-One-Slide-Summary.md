# URGLDN – One-Slide Summary

## The Problem
44% of Canadians say money stress is their #1 stressor.  
Most issues aren’t caused by income — they’re caused by **timing failures**:
- Bills hitting before payday  
- Forgotten payments  
- Auto-pay pulling when accounts aren’t ready  
- Small shortfalls causing overdrafts, late fees, and license suspensions  

## The Solution
**URGLDN – Lock & Release™**
- Locks bill money early  
- Protects it from accidental spending  
- Releases it automatically at the exact due time  
- Works inside existing banking apps  
- Gives users true spending money and peace of mind
