# Founder Bio – Shelby Nicolle Isaacs  
*Creator of URGLDN – A Timing-First Financial Wellness Tool*

My name is Shelby Isaacs, and I built URGLDN out of personal frustration and lived experience. I come from a middle‑class family in Brandon, Manitoba, where I graduated high school and worked steady jobs most of my life. Everything changed over the last few years when I went through a difficult relationship, a major life hardship, and the long recovery that followed. I was forced onto disability while dealing with PTSD, and during that time I learned firsthand how hard it is to manage bills, avoid overspending, and stay financially stable on a fixed income.

One moment changed everything for me:  
I was **two dollars short** on my insurance payment.  
A small $2 charge came out of my account without warning.  
I didn’t know.  
I couldn’t check my banking app because I was out of service range.  
And because of that tiny shortfall, my license was suspended for the rest of the year.

On top of that, the bank charged me **two NSF fees of $45 each** because the system tried twice to pull the payment.  
All over two dollars.

It was heartbreaking — and I knew I wasn’t the only one. I’ve heard countless stories of people losing their license or getting hit with fees because a payment didn’t go through on time, even when the money was there. Timing failures ruin people’s lives, and the system doesn’t protect them.

That’s when I asked myself:  
**Why can’t this problem be eliminated?**  
Why can’t we have a system that pays bills *ahead of time*, protects the money, and releases it exactly when it’s needed?  
Why can’t we have *smart pay* — something that does the job for you?

I always wanted to pay my bills early so I wouldn’t accidentally spend the money, but banks don’t offer that. There was no way to lock funds safely and still have them released at the right moment.

So I built the idea myself.

URGLDN is the tool I needed — and the tool millions of people need.  
It locks bill money early, protects it from accidental spending, and releases it automatically at the exact right time. It gives people peace of mind, structure, and confidence, especially those living with stress, disability, fixed income, or unpredictable life circumstances.

I came up with the idea almost exactly a year ago and have been working on it ever since. I secured the copyright in November 2025, built an early‑stage working prototype, and developed the full product documentation, architecture, roadmap, branding, and valuation model.

I’m not a developer — I’m the user this platform was made for.  
I know the struggle.  
I know the stress.  
And I know the difference this tool can make.

I’m looking for a partner who believes in this vision as much as I do — someone who sees the potential to change lives, modernize financial wellness, and build something meaningful. I’m open to licensing, co‑development, and equity structures, with a royalty model that keeps me tied to URGLDN’s long‑term success.

URGLDN was built from lived experience, not theory.  
It’s built to protect people from timing failures.  
It’s built to give peace of mind.  
It’s built to help people stay golden.

— **Shelby Isaacs**  
Founder, URGLDN
