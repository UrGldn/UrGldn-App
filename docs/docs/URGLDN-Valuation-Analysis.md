## 2. Market Need

Money stress is the #1 stressor in Canada.  
**44% of Canadians** say it is the biggest source of stress in their lives, and this has been rising for three consecutive years.

The core issue is not budgeting — it is **timing**:

- Bills hitting before payday  
- Forgotten payments  
- Auto‑pay pulling when accounts aren’t ready  
- Overspending before a bill is due  

This leads to overdrafts, late fees, missed payments, and damaged credit.

URGLDN solves this with a simple, automated timing system.
