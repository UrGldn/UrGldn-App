What URGLDN Is
URGLDN is a financial technology tool that integrates directly into existing banking apps.
It works like autopay — but smarter.

Instead of pulling funds blindly on the due date, URGLDN uses a Lock‑and‑Release™ mechanism:

Locks bill money ahead of time

Protects it from accidental spending

Releases it back to the main account exactly when the bill is due

This ensures:

Bills are always paid on time

Late fees are eliminated

Overdraft risk is reduced

Users build or maintain strong credit

What’s left in the account becomes true spending money

URGLDN gives people financial peace through timing, not budgeting.

The Problem
Money stress is Canada’s #1 stressor — 44% of Canadians say it’s the biggest source of stress in their lives.

The issue isn’t always income.
It’s timing.

People struggle because:

Bills come out before payday

One payment gets forgotten

Auto‑pay pulls when the account isn’t ready

Overspending happens before a bill hits

This leads to:

Overdraft fees

Late fees

Missed payments

Damaged credit

Constant anxiety

URGLDN removes that stress.
Set it once — the system handles the timing.

The Solution
URGLDN acts like a personal bill‑timing assistant:

Users link their existing bank accounts (via secure APIs like Plaid or Flinks)

They create “vaults” for each bill

URGLDN locks the money early

The system releases it at the exact right moment

What’s left in the account becomes guilt‑free spending money — because the bills are already handled.

This is especially powerful for people who:

Struggle with overspending

Live paycheck‑to‑paycheck

Have irregular income

Are rebuilding credit

Are overwhelmed by timing and organization

URGLDN gives them a clean, automated structure that removes the mental load.
