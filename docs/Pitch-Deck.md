# Slide 2 — The Problem
Millions of Canadians aren’t struggling because they don’t earn enough —  
they’re struggling because **bill timing doesn’t match income timing**.

This leads to:
- Overdraft fees  
- Late fees  
- Missed payments  
- Damaged credit  
- Constant financial stress  

Existing tools (reminders, calendars, auto‑pay) rely on user discipline or pull funds when the account isn’t ready.

Banks feel the impact through:
- High complaint volume  
- Customer churn  
- Negative satisfaction scores  

---

# Slide 3 — The Insight
The real issue isn’t budgeting.  
It’s **timing**.

People don’t need another reminder.  
They need a system that **protects their money from themselves** and releases it at the right moment.

---

# Slide 4 — The URGLDN Solution
URGLDN is a **virtual banking assistant** that:

- **Locks** bill money ahead of time  
- **Protects** it from accidental spending  
- **Releases** it back to the main account **exactly when the bill is due**  

It works like auto‑pay — but smarter, safer, and timing‑aware.

---

# Slide 5 — How It Works
**1. Create a Vault**  
User sets a bill, amount, and due date.

**2. Lock Funds**  
Money is moved into a protected vault.

**3. Release on Schedule**  
Funds are automatically released at the exact right time.

**4. Reset for Next Cycle**  
The vault prepares for the next payment.

This eliminates:
- Late fees  
- Overdrafts  
- Surprise withdrawals
